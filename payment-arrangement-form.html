Option A — Late Fees Waived Upon Completion. All accrued late fees included in the outstanding balance shall be waived only upon Tenant’s full and timely completion of all payments under this Agreement. If Tenant defaults on any payment, all previously waived late fees shall be immediately reinstated and become due and payable.
Option B — Late Fees Continue to Accrue. Late fees shall continue to accrue on any unpaid balance in accordance with the terms of the Lease Agreement until the total outstanding balance is paid in full. Any additional late fees incurred during the term of this Agreement shall be added to the outstanding balance.
Section VII

Terms You Are Agreeing To

Please read each clause carefully and acknowledge below.

1. Landlord’s Right to Cancel. Landlord reserves the right to cancel this Payment Arrangement Agreement at any time and for any reason prior to the outstanding balance being paid in full. Upon cancellation, the entire remaining outstanding balance shall become immediately due and payable, and Landlord may pursue any and all remedies available under the Lease Agreement and applicable law, including but not limited to the filing of an eviction action.

2. Default. A default under this Agreement shall occur if Tenant: (a) fails to make any arrangement payment on or before the scheduled due date; (b) fails to pay current rent in full and on time; or (c) otherwise violates any term of the Lease Agreement. Upon default, the entire remaining balance shall become immediately due and payable, and Landlord may pursue all available legal remedies without further notice.

3. Courtesy Agreement Disclaimer. Tenant acknowledges and agrees that this payment arrangement is a voluntary courtesy extended by the Landlord and is not required by law or by the Lease Agreement. This Agreement has been offered by Landlord in an effort to assist Tenant in resolving the outstanding balance and to avoid the necessity of filing an eviction action. However, Landlord expressly retains the right to file for eviction at any time in accordance with applicable law, including during the term of this Agreement, if Landlord determines in its sole discretion that such action is warranted. This Agreement does not constitute a waiver of any rights or remedies available to Landlord under the Lease Agreement or South Carolina law.

4. General Provisions. This Agreement does not modify, amend, or replace the Lease Agreement in any way. All terms and conditions of the Lease Agreement remain in full force and effect. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of South Carolina. This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties with respect to the payment arrangement described herein and supersedes any prior oral or written discussions regarding the same. Any modifications to this Agreement must be in writing and signed by both parties.

What happens next

  1. The property manager will review your proposal, typically within 3–5 business days.
  2. You will receive an email response indicating approval, requested modifications, or denial.
  3. If approved, you will receive a final agreement to sign before the arrangement takes effect.
  4. Until you receive written approval, continue meeting all obligations under your existing Lease Agreement, including timely payment of full monthly rent.
